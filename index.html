redNAND guide

Complete Wii U redNAND guide, from stock to redNAND.

What's redNAND?

redNAND stands for redirected NAND, the usual NAND would be loaded from the Wii U's internal chip, but after installing this you can have it load from your sd card.

What can I do with redNAND?

Having a redNAND gives you the ability to install custom apps, system config tool, and a little bit more ;).
